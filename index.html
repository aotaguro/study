Welcome To StudySort!

Calendar:

A simple calendar tool to manage and view events. To make an event click on a cell and input a name. To delete any events right click on top of them. Use the buttons on the bottom to navigate

StudyMode:

A stopwatch-style studying clock that alerts you after 30 min of studying to give yourself a short break. Use start, stop, and reset features to perform their respective actions.

Notepad:

A simple notepad with creating, editing, and deleting features with their respective buttons.

StudyPlanner:

Set reminders to alert you to do certain tasks. Use the dropdown menu to select dates and times for you to get notified. Make sure to name your events and hit the add event button. You can delete the events that you create by right-clicking on them.
